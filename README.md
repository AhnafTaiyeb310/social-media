# Aura

Welcome to Aura, a full-stack social media platform that connects individuals and communities in meaningful ways. Our mission is to provide a seamless user experience that fosters engagement, creativity, and connection among users. 

## Features:
- **User Profiles**: Create and customize your profile to reflect your interests and personality.
- **Post Sharing**: Share your thoughts, images, and videos with your network.
- **Real-Time Messaging**: Connect with friends through instant messaging.
- **Community Groups**: Join groups that match your interests and participate in discussions.
- **Privacy Control**: Maintain control over your data with customizable privacy settings.

## Technology Stack:
- **Frontend**: Built using React.js for a dynamic user interface.
- **Backend**: Powered by Node.js and Express, ensuring robust performance.
- **Database**: Utilizes MongoDB for efficient data storage and retrieval.
- **Real-Time Features**: Incorporates WebSocket for live messaging capabilities.

Stay tuned for updates as we grow Aura into the premier platform for social interaction!
